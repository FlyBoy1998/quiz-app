Rules

Ok, so the rules are pretty simple! After you press the start button, you have to select one of the difficulty levels(easy, medium, or hard). Depending on the difficulty level selected, the conditions are as follows:

  1. Easy level - 10 questions with 25 seconds to answer each question. For each correct answer you'll receive 100 points! For each wrong answer you'll lose 50 points!
  2. Medium level - 15 questions with 20 seconds to answer each question. For each correct answer you'll receive 100 points! For each wrong answer you'll lose 75 points!
  3. Hard level - 20 questions with 15 seconds to answer each question. For each correct answer you'll receive 100 points! For each wrong answer you'll lose 100 points!

After you finish answering all the questions, a new window will pop-up showing your score and your top 3 best scores!

Good luck and have fun!
